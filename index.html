VroomCast



Installation

  • 1. If you have an old version of VroomCast, remove the old version by going to Chrome's extensions page ( chrome://extensions/ ) and clicking the trash can next to the VroomCast app and VroomCast Companion extension. After removing the app and extension, restart your computer.
  • 2. Install the VroomCast Companion Chrome extension
  • 3. Install the VroomCast Chrome app

Common Problems

Problem: I hear ringing in the background while I'm on a call. How can I make it stop?
Solution: This probably means you have Talkdesk open in a browser tab. To make the ringing stop, you can mute the tab(s) which have Talkdesk open by right-clicking the top of the tab and selecting "Mute Tab"

Problem: VroomCast isn't telling me whether the caller is expecting Vroom or TDA when I answer the phone.
Solution: VroomCast will not always be able to tell you what the customer is expecting if they call you directly. We can only inform you which brand to say if the person is calling a number on one of our ads that is associated with one of our brands (e.g. Vroom-Craisglist-Chicago).

Problem: My headset doesn't appear to be working or my customers cannot hear me.
Solution: Please ensure your headset's USB cable is plugged in and the headset is configured with Windows as the default audio source. If you need help, please send an email describing the problem to helpdesk@texasdirectauto.com

Problem: When clicking the button to transfer a call, I get an error that says "transfer failed".
Solution: We are aware of this issue; until it is sorted out, you can manually type in the phone number you wish to transfer the call to.

Problem: I can't log in to VroomCast or VroomCast isn't working properly.
Solution: Sign out of all Gmail and Google accounts, then sign back into your Vroom Gmail account. Follow the instructions above to re-install VroomCast. If this doesn't work, describe your problem in the #vroomcast-feedback channel on Slack or email helpdesk@texasdirectauto.com

Searching for a car

You can search for a car by year/make/model or by VIN. Click the V icon to search Vroom.com or
click the flag icon to search TDA.com.



Editing or updating customer info

Sometimes we need to update customer information or create it for new customers.
You can do this directly in VroomCast. All changes will be saved to Base/DMS/Back Office
Note: it takes about 5 minutes for the save to take effect.



Searching for a customer

If a current customer is calling from a new number, or if the user information is not correct,
we can search our database for the correct customer record.

You can search for a person by name or email address. To search by email, type out the entire email address.



Opening a customer in Base or Back Office

You can open a customer's page on Base or Back Office by clicking the blue buttons that say "Base" and "BO".
